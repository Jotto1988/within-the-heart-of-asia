5 Ways to Experience Seoul from Home
Oct 26, 2025
https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1538485399081-7191377e8241?q=80&w=1000
You don't need a plane ticket to feel the heartbeat of Korea. Here is how I am immersing myself in Seoul culture while saving for my dream trip. 1. The Virtual Museum Tour Did you know the National Museum of Korea offers VR tours? I spent hours walking through the Joseon Dynasty halls yesterday. It was breathtaking to see the crowns and pottery up close, even through a screen. 2. The Kitchen Challenge I finally tried making Kimchi Jjigae. The secret is aged Kimchi! It smells pungent but tastes like heaven. 3. K-Drama Language Learning I stopped using subtitles for the simple phrases. Hearing "Annyeonghaseyo" in Crash Landing on You hits different when you are studying. 4. K-Pop Online Concerts Nothing beats the energy of a live stream at 3 AM with millions of other fans. The chat rooms go wild and you feel connected to the world. 5. Planning the Itinerary I'm using Naver Maps to pin my future coffee spots in Hongdae.
